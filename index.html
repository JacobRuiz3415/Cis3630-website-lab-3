what are the problems of transportation in LA


Car denpendency

Having car is often the main requirement for many people. most infrastructure is design mostly with car in main. this can make city hard to walk around.

The cost of having a car

Buying and maintaining a car is very expensive. This can be a financial burden for people on low-income. some family may not be able to buy a car, so they need public transit to move around.

Environmental costs

Car produce green house emissions via exhaust. manufacturing car costs resources and produce emission in the process. Electric car would also have their own environmental costs.

Underfunded Public Transportation

Public transportation is underfunded. public transit in many cities in the U.S. face funding deficits. this can led cut in services that public transit can provide.


What are the benefits from having better public transportation


The city is more walkable

People can travel travel more easily without of the use of a car.


Support for low-income families

Health Benefits

City being more walkable can improve public health. people can have more access to health services and health food

Environmental sustainably

Fewer cars would lead to less green house emissions. According to the World Resources Institute, public transit, such as buses and trains, can reduce greenhouse gas by two-thirds compared to passenger vehicles.
